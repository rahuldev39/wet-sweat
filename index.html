Make A Vet Sweat (MAVS) is a nonprofit organization committed to serving Veterans who are combating the hidden wounds of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).

We accomplish this objective by sponsoring our applicants at their local gyms for three month periods to sweat out the toxins of PTSD’s with like-minded people.

Ready to take the next step and join an active community of Veterans and other supporters who are passionate about our mission? We’re waiting to hear from you.

THE ORIGIN STORY

Our mission, it is sustained through donations and partnerships, with the most notable of these being our annual MAVS Charity Classic.

In 2016 we held our inaugural MAVS Charity Classic, which is not only sanctioned by the Naturally Fit Federation, but is also a Pro Qualifier.

The funds raised through events such as this is what gives Make A Vet Sweat the ability fight PTSD while being an advocate for countless Veterans.
